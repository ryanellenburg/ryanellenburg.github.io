Why is your page so... unsophisticated?

Couple reasons. First, I'm not much of a narcissist, but I was required to make a home page to bring attention to myself (coding training project). Requirement was without the use of web design software. Also, having the luxury of saying I went to Georgia Tech in a previous millennium, we learned HTML long before the days of WordPress, Webflow and Elementor. While I like making modern pages and apps, I'm good with this one sticking to my OG roots.
